    • Description
      An ATS facility is characterized by a shallow, flat, inclined (0.5-2% slope) exposed surface (the floway) (Fig. 1A), either roughened or lined with a high surface-area fabric to provide a growth substratum. A wave- or pulse-generator directs influent into the floway along its upper contour (Fig. 1A,B), producing a shallow (2-5 cm (0.7 - 2 in) deep) flow coursing downslope. This situation is compounded by the fact that agriculture is a low-margin enterprise.
